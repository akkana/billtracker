The New Mexico Bill Tracker is a free service to help you track the progress of legislative bills you're interested in. It can tell you when a bill's status changes -- like when it moves to a new committee, or to the House or Senate floor for a vote. If you register with an email address, it can send you updates when any of your bills is scheduled for discussion.

See more things it can do on the Help page.
